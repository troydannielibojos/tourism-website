Gigantes Islands

Hidden in Iloilo, Gigantes is a paradise of turquoise waters, towering limestone cliffs, and untouched white sand beaches.

Why Visit Gigantes?

🏝 Island Hopping

Explore multiple islands in one trip with breathtaking views everywhere you go.

🍤 Fresh Seafood

Enjoy affordable and freshly caught seafood like scallops and crabs.

📸 Scenic Spots

Perfect Instagram-worthy locations like Cabugao Gamay viewpoint.

📅 Best Time to Visit

November to May offers calm seas and sunny weather.

📍 Location

Carles, Iloilo, Philippines — accessible by boat tours.

💡 Travel Tip

Book early during peak season to secure island hopping tours.
